About Me

Rayan is a Security Engineer III at Comcast, where he leads Zero Trust micro-segmentation, cloud automation, and incident response across AWS, Azure, and Openstack. He previously worked as a Firewall Engineer at Susquehanna International Group (SIG), and has interned on security and network engineering teams throughout his career.

Rayan is currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at Georgia Institute of Technology. He earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Drexel University, with a minor in Finance. During his undergraduate studies, he participated in a study abroad program in France at INSA Lyon and founded the Nepali Student Association at Drexel. His technical interests span cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, network security, and machine learning.

Outside of work, Rayan enjoys solo traveling (16 countries, 44 cities), playing soccer, table tennis, basketball, tennis, and pickleball, exploring new restaurants, gaming, and attending live music concerts.
